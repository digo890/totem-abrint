## 3. Typography

### Font Families

| Font | Usage | Weights |
|---|---|---|
| **Poppins** | Website, editorial design, all brand materials | Light · Regular · Italic · Medium · Semi-Bold |
| **Arial** | Word documents, PowerPoint presentations (editable files) | Regular · Italic · Bold |

> **Rule:** Never use fonts outside these two families. Never rotate, deform, stretch, or convert to outline.

### Type Scale (Poppins)

| Role | Size | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display / Currency | `3.5rem` (56px) | Bold | Hero numbers, bill amounts — always the focal point |
| Headline Large | `2.25rem` (36px) | Medium | Section headers |
| Headline Medium | `1.5rem` (24px) | Medium | Sub-section headers |
| Body | `0.875rem` (14px) | Regular | Product descriptions, plan details, body copy |
| Label / Signature | `0.6875rem` (11px) | Light | Uppercase · `letter-spacing: 0.05em` · metadata |

### Typography Rules

- Do not place text over people's faces or eyes
- Do not use white text on light backgrounds
- Do not use dark text on dark backgrounds
- Do not place text on photographs with excessive detail
- Numbers are always the hero of the screen — use display scale for financial figures

## 4. Logotype

### Isotype Construction
The Bemobi symbol is composed of **11 triangles** arranged from a base equilateral triangle grid. The construction steps: equilateral triangle → rotate 90° → duplicate and mirror horizontally → stack in zigzag → duplicate overlapping the vertices.

### Logo Variants

| Variant | Background | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Primary horizontal (color) | Light backgrounds | Default — all communications |
| Horizontal black | Light backgrounds | B&W / grayscale printing, formal documents |
| Horizontal white type (color isotype + white text) | Dark, gradients, photos | Primary dark-mode usage |
| Horizontal white (all white) | Dark, gradients, photos | Secondary dark-mode usage |
| Vertical (same 4 variants) | Same as above | Vertical format compositions |
| Isotype only | Any | When logotype use would be redundant |

### Spacing & Protection

- Protection space = **X** (defined by the typography height of the logotype)
- Horizontal logo: protection space is derived from **2 triangles** of the isotype grid
- Vertical logo: separation between isotype and type = **1 triangle** of the grid
- Isotype alone: protection space = **X** on all sides

### Partner Lockup

When pairing with another brand logo: separate with a vertical dividing line, with **X** space on each side of the line.

### Logo Don'ts

- Do not compress, stretch, or distort
- Do not change letter-spacing
- Do not change colors outside the official variants
- Do not convert to linear outline
- Do not apply glows, reflections, or graphic effects
- Do not rotate
- Do not dismantle the isotype triangles
- Do not apply unofficial gradients
- Do not place on backgrounds that hinder legibility
- No elements may intrude on the clear space

## 5. Graphic Elements

### Bemobi Pattern

A geometric background pattern built from the same triangle grid as the isotype. Available in:

**Color variants:** Black & White · Deep Purple · Light Purple · Light Blue · Dark Blue · Purple & Cyan

**Format variants per color:**
- Square application
- Slim horizontal band
- Thick horizontal band

**Usage:** Backgrounds for graphic pieces, presentation slides, brand communications.

### Perpendicular Lines

Diagonal intersecting lines that create color zones — used as full backgrounds or side elements in presentations and posts.

### Image Crop Shapes

| Shape | Use Case |
|---|---|
| Hexagon | Infographics, diagrams, presentations |
| Diagonal | Conceptual graphics, mosaics |
| SIM Card | Product/service diagrams, presentations |
| Isotype | Advertising graphics, brand content |

## 6. Imagery

### Photography Style

- **Sharp** and high-contrast
- **Vibrant, colorful** tones — never flat or desaturated
- **Authentic** — real interactions, not staged stock
- Faces must be **fully visible** — never crop eyes or faces
- Attitudes must be **optimistic and cheerful** — never frustrated or pessimistic
- No blurry, pixelated, overexposed, or excessively saturated photos
- No watermarks in official materials
- **Multicultural** — represent users from various world cultures and generations

### Image Don'ts

- No images unrelated to Bemobi's conceptual world
- No excessively dark images
- No overexposed / oversaturated photos
- No negative or pessimistic user attitudes
- No crops that hide user actions

## 7. Tone of Voice

### Communication Style — Do

| Trait | Description |
|---|---|
| Objective | Get straight to the point |
| Empathetic | From people to people |
| Professional | Approachable but not overly intimate |
| Inclusive | Consider various groups |
| Motivational | Demonstrate that overcoming challenges is possible |
| Innovative | Go beyond the comfort zone |
| Articulate | Repertoire aligned with experience |
| Instructive | Simplify without excluding |
| Serious | Investment and attention are important topics |
| Global | Use English terms when relevant |
| Impersonal | Treat everyone equally, no preferences |

### Communication Style — Don't

| Avoid | Reason |
|---|---|
| Intimate | Don't cross the professional boundary |
| Humorous | Investments and partnerships are serious |
| Blunt | We understand customers' issues |
| Exclusive | We include minority groups |
| Outdated | We follow digital trends |
| Dismissive | Never assume people know everything |
| Arrogant | Be a leader without belittling competitors |
| Emoji | Avoid in investor / B2B communications |

### Negative Keywords (never use as direction)

Conservative · Rigid · Authoritarian · Traditional · Cold · Comedian · Political · Rough

### Positive Keywords (use as direction)

Agility · Trust · Efficiency · Impactful · Objectivity · Responsibility · Modern · Ethical · Leader · Solidity · Articulation · Honesty · Inclusion · Collaboration · Reference · Experience · Innovation · Knowledge · Multidisciplinary · Virtue · Unity · Research · Impulse

### Key Brand Messages

- *"Our purpose is to build a more inclusive digital world through mobile solutions."*
- *"We are the drive for companies to overcome challenges."*
- *"We take you where others believe to be unreachable."*
- *"Bemobi: your bridge to success."*
- *"We deliver solutions that embrace audiences, regardless of where they are."*
- *"At Bemobi, innovation and strategy travel together."*
- *"Effortless Payments, Endless Possibilities."*

## 8. Audiences & Tone Adaptation

| Audience | Tone |
|---|---|
| **Investors** | Knowledge + transparency · Present real data directly · No complex jargon · Accommodating for all questions |
| **Companies** | Clever + efficient · Professional + impartial · Modest about competition · No familiarity |
| **End Customers** | Empathetic throughout · Humanized messages · Accessible + inclusive language · Gender-neutral wording |

**Accessibility rules (all audiences):**
- Use descriptive alt text for all images
- Use contextual URLs (not "click here" — use "Access the user manual here")
- Use gender-neutral language ("Well done, everyone" not "Well done, guys")

## 9. Video & Motion

### Video Components

| Element | Usage |
|---|---|
| **Title screen** | Opening of video or to separate content blocks |
| **Transition screen 1** | Diagonal slide transition (4 color variants) |
| **Transition screen 2** | Wipe transition (Blue / Cyan / Purple / Gradient) |
| **Lower title** | Name and title of speakers — font sizes must not be altered |
| **Outro** | Closing screen — person + diagonal shape + Bemobi logo |

### Video General Rules

- Apply image guidelines (section 6) to all stock footage
- Prefer smooth camera movements — no handheld shakiness
- Music must enhance, not distract
- Logo must always be visible using official MOGRT horizontal or vertical variants
- Maximum 2 color palettes per video to avoid visual overload
- Include subtitles (hearing accessibility) and voiceover when possible (visual accessibility)
